Frequently Asked Questions

How much does masonry work cost?

Every project is unique. Costs depend on the type of stone or brick, the size of the project, and site conditions. We provide detailed free estimates so you know exactly what to expect — no surprises.

What types of stone and brick do you work with?

We work with natural limestone, flagstone, fieldstone, manufactured stone veneer, standard brick, and concrete block. We'll help you choose the right material for your project's style and budget.

How long does a typical masonry project take?

Most residential projects like tree rings, planters, or small retaining walls take 1-3 days. Larger projects like patios or full retaining wall systems may take a week or more. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes. We stand behind every project we complete. Our workmanship is built to last through Texas heat, rain, and freeze cycles.

Do I need a permit for masonry work?

Most decorative masonry like planters and tree rings doesn't require permits. For retaining walls over 4 feet or structural work, permits may be needed — we handle that process for you.
